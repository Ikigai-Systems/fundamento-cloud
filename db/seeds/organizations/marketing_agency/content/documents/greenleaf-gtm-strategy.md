# GreenLeaf Organics - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Overview

GreenLeaf Organics is expanding their direct-to-consumer presence through a multi-channel social media strategy. This document outlines our approach to driving brand awareness, engagement, and conversions for their organic food product line across Q1-Q2.

## Market Analysis

The organic food market continues to grow at 12% annually. Consumer research shows our target demographic increasingly discovers new brands through social media, with Instagram and TikTok driving the highest purchase intent in the health and wellness category. Key competitors (EarthBite, PureRoots) are investing heavily in influencer partnerships but underperforming on community engagement -- an opportunity we plan to exploit.

## Target Audience

- **Primary**: Health-conscious millennials (28-38), urban, household income $75K+
- **Secondary**: Gen Z consumers (22-27) entering the organic market for the first time
- **Tertiary**: Parents (30-45) seeking healthier options for their families

## Channel Strategy

| Channel | Focus | Posting Cadence |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Instagram | Product lifestyle, recipes, UGC | 5x/week |
| TikTok | Short-form recipe videos, trends | 3x/week |
| Facebook | Community building, events | 3x/week |
| Pinterest | Recipe pins, meal prep boards | 2x/week |

## Budget Allocation

Total quarterly budget: **$48,000**

- Paid social ads: 40% ($19,200)
- Influencer partnerships: 25% ($12,000)
- Content production: 20% ($9,600)
- Community management: 15% ($7,200)

1. **Reach**: 2M total impressions per quarter
2. **Engagement**: Maintain 4.5%+ engagement rate across platforms
3. **Growth**: 15% follower growth month-over-month
4. **Conversions**: 500 trackable website visits per week from social
5. **Sentiment**: Maintain 90%+ positive sentiment score
