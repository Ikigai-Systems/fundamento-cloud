# Campaign Brainstorm - Spring Collection Ideas

Raw ideas from our February brainstorm session. Nothing is finalized here -- this is a dumping ground. Star marks the ones we want to explore further.

---

## GreenLeaf Organics - Spring Menu Launch

- "Spring Reset" campaign tied to seasonal eating, detox-without-calling-it-detox angle
- Partner with a nutritionist for a 7-day meal plan using GreenLeaf ingredients -- could be an Instagram guide or downloadable PDF
- * Farmer spotlight series: short videos visiting their actual suppliers. Authenticity play. Could do really well on TikTok
- Earth Day tie-in (April 22) -- limited edition packaging, plant-a-tree partnership? Check if client has sustainability goals we can lean into
- * Recipe challenge: "5 Ingredients, 15 Minutes" user-generated content push. Simple hashtag, low barrier to entry, great for engagement
- Before/after fridge makeover content -- show the switch from processed to organic in a fun, non-judgmental way
- Easter brunch content -- easy seasonal hook, carousel format with recipe cards
- Spring farmers market pop-up activation? Would need client buy-in and budget. Probably a stretch for Q2 but worth flagging

## UrbanFit - Summer Collection Launch

- * "City Summer" campaign: urban outdoor workouts, rooftop yoga, park runs. Show the gear in real city environments
- Heat-focused product messaging: breathable fabrics, moisture-wicking, designed for hot days
- * Collab with a running club or cycling group for a branded group workout event. Film it for content. Gets us UGC and community engagement in one shot
- Summer styling guide: workout-to-brunch outfit transitions. Carousel or Reel format
- "What's in my gym bag" influencer series -- send products to 10 micro-influencers, let them create unboxing content their way
- Countdown to summer series starting in April -- build anticipation for the collection drop
- * Partner with a fitness app for a 30-day summer challenge. Cross-promotion opportunity

## General Spring Themes

- Spring cleaning for your content strategy (meta/educational angle for our own brand)
- Daylight savings: "extra hour of daylight = extra hour to move" -- quick social post idea
- Outdoor content push across all clients as weather improves
- Refresh visual templates with spring color palette -- lighter, brighter tones
- Q1 wrap-up case study content: share wins (with client permission) to attract new prospects
