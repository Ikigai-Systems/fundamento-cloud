# Vacation and PTO Policy

## Overview

Time off is important. We want everyone at BrightPath to take real breaks so they come back recharged and ready to do their best work. This policy covers paid time off (PTO), including vacation days, personal days, and sick leave.

## Accrual Rates

- **Year 1**: 15 days of PTO per calendar year
- **After 2 years**: 20 days of PTO per calendar year
- PTO accrues on a per-pay-period basis starting from your hire date

Sick leave is separate and follows local regulations. If you are unsure about your balance, check with Sarah.

## Requesting Time Off

1. Submit your PTO request **at least two weeks in advance** for planned absences
2. Add the request to the Vacation Tracker table in this space
3. Your manager will approve or discuss adjustments within two business days
4. Once approved, block the dates on your calendar and update your Slack status

For unplanned absences (illness, emergencies), notify your manager and the team Slack channel as soon as possible.

## Blackout Dates

We ask that team members avoid scheduling PTO during **major client launches** without prior manager approval. These are high-stakes windows where the full team needs to be available. If you are unsure whether a launch window applies to you, check with your account manager or Sarah.

Outside of launch windows, we do our best to accommodate all requests. If multiple people request the same dates, we handle it on a first-come, first-served basis and work together to find alternatives.

## Rollover Policy

You may roll over a **maximum of 5 unused PTO days** into the next calendar year. Any days beyond 5 will expire on December 31. We encourage you to use your time -- it is there for a reason.

## Holidays

BrightPath observes standard US federal holidays. The full list is shared at the beginning of each year.

## Questions

If anything about this policy is unclear, reach out to Sarah. We want this to be straightforward, not a source of stress.
