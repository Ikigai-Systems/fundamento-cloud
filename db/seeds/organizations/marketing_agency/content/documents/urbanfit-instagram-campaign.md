# UrbanFit - Instagram Campaign Plan

## Campaign: "Move Your Way" Spring/Summer

**Client**: UrbanFit
**Platform**: Instagram (Feed, Stories, Reels)
**Duration**: March - June
**Lead**: Elena Vasquez
**Creative**: Marcus Thompson

## Content Pillars

### 1. Workout Tips (30% of content)
Quick, actionable fitness content that provides real value. Think "3 stretches you can do at your desk" or "5-minute morning mobility routine." Always feature UrbanFit gear naturally in the shots without making it the focus.

### 2. Product Showcases (25% of content)
Highlight new arrivals, seasonal collections, and hero products. Focus on how pieces fit into real life -- commuting, training, brunch after a workout. Avoid traditional product-on-white-background shots. Everything should feel in-context and aspirational but attainable.

### 3. Community Stories (25% of content)
Feature real UrbanFit customers and their fitness journeys. Source UGC through the #MoveYourWay hashtag and reach out for permission to repost. Also includes athlete and micro-influencer spotlights.

### 4. Behind the Scenes (20% of content)
Give followers a look at UrbanFit's process -- product design, fabric testing, photo shoots, team workouts. This humanizes the brand and builds emotional connection.

## Posting Schedule

| Day | Format | Pillar |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Monday | Reel | Workout Tips |
| Tuesday | Carousel | Product Showcase |
| Wednesday | Stories (3-5 slides) | Behind the Scenes |
| Thursday | Reel | Community Stories |
| Friday | Single image or carousel | Product or Community |

**Total**: 5 feed posts per week + daily Stories

## Engagement Strategy

- Respond to every comment within 4 hours during business hours
- Like and comment on tagged UGC posts daily
- Run weekly polls and question stickers in Stories to drive interaction
- Host a monthly Instagram Live with a fitness creator (coordinate with influencer partners)
- DM first-time taggers with a thank-you message and discount code

## Influencer Partnerships

**Tier 1 (2 creators, 100K+ followers)**: Monthly sponsored posts + Reels. Focus on lifestyle integration, not hard sells. Budget: $3,000/month each.

**Tier 2 (5 creators, 10K-50K followers)**: Product seeding with optional paid amplification for top performers. Budget: $500/month each.

**Tier 3 (ongoing)**: Community-driven. Repost organic UGC from customers with proper credit. No direct cost.

## Metrics and Targets

| Metric | Monthly Target |
|--------|---------------|
| Follower growth | +2,500 net new |
| Engagement rate | 4.0%+ |
| Reel views (average) | 25,000 |
| Story completion rate | 70%+ |
| Link clicks to site | 3,000 |
| DMs received | 200+ |

## Reporting

Elena will compile weekly metrics snapshots in the Content Calendar and deliver a full monthly performance report to Priya for the client check-in. Any metric falling below 80% of target gets flagged immediately so we can adjust.
