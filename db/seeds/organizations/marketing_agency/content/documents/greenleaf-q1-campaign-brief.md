# GreenLeaf Organics - Q1 Campaign Brief

## Campaign Overview

**Client**: GreenLeaf Organics
**Campaign**: "Fresh Start" Q1 Launch
**Duration**: January 15 - March 31
**Lead**: James Rivera
**Account Manager**: Priya Patel

## Objective

Drive awareness and trial of GreenLeaf's new organic meal kit line among health-conscious millennials. Primary goal is to generate 10,000 website visits from social channels and convert 500 first-time purchases through tracked promo codes.

## Target Audience

**Primary segment**: Health-conscious millennials, ages 28-38

- Urban professionals who value convenience but care about what they eat
- Active on Instagram and TikTok, follow food and wellness creators
- Willing to pay a premium for organic, sustainably sourced products
- Pain point: want to cook healthy meals but struggle with time and planning

## Key Messages

1. "Real ingredients, real simple" -- meal kits remove the friction from healthy cooking
2. "From farm to your kitchen in 3 steps" -- emphasize sourcing transparency and ease
3. "Your weeknight reset" -- position meal kits as the antidote to unhealthy takeout habits

## Visual Direction

- **Color palette**: Earthy tones -- sage green, warm cream, terracotta accents
- **Photography style**: Natural light, overhead flat-lays of ingredients and finished meals
- **Avoid**: Overly polished or sterile food photography. We want warmth and authenticity.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif for headlines, handwritten accents for callouts
- Reference moodboard is in the shared Google Drive folder (link in Slack #greenleaf)

- 2M+ total impressions across platforms
- 4.5%+ average engagement rate
- 10,000 website clicks via social (tracked with UTM parameters)
- 500 first-time purchases using campaign promo code FRESHSTART
- Cost per acquisition under $18
