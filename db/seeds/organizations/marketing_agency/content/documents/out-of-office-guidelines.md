# Out of Office Guidelines

## Why This Matters

When you are out of office, your clients and teammates need to know who to contact and what is being covered. A smooth handoff prevents dropped balls and keeps client work on track. Take five minutes before you leave to set everything up properly.

## Before You Leave

### 1. Update Your Slack Status

Set your Slack status to "OOO" with your return date. Example: "OOO - back Feb 24". Turn on Do Not Disturb if you prefer not to receive notifications.

### 2. Block Your Calendar

Mark your calendar as out of office for the full duration. Decline or reschedule any meetings that fall during your absence.

### 3. Brief Your Backup

Identify a backup for each active client or project and walk them through:

- Current status of deliverables
- Upcoming deadlines during your absence
- Any pending client feedback or approvals
- Where to find relevant files and assets

### 4. Set Your Email Auto-Reply

Use this template or adapt it to your style:

> Subject: Out of Office
>
> Thanks for your email. I am currently out of the office from [start date] through [return date] with limited access to email.
>
> For urgent BrightPath matters, please contact [backup name] at [backup email]. For general inquiries, I will respond when I return.
>
> Best regards,
> [Your name]

### 5. Notify Active Clients

If you manage client relationships directly, send a brief heads-up to your primary contacts at least a few days before your absence. Introduce them to your backup so they are not caught off guard.

## Handoff Checklist for Active Client Work

- [ ] List all active campaigns and their current status
- [ ] Share login credentials or access to scheduling tools with your backup
- [ ] Flag any content that needs approval while you are away
- [ ] Note any meetings your backup should attend on your behalf
- [ ] Confirm your backup has access to the relevant client project spaces

## When You Return

- Check in with your backup for a quick debrief
- Review any comments or updates in your project documents
- Update your Slack status back to active
- Follow up on any client communications that came in during your absence

## Coverage Expectations

We do not expect anyone to check messages while on PTO. That said, if something truly urgent comes up, your backup will reach out via text or phone as a last resort. Define "truly urgent" with your manager before you leave so there are no surprises.
