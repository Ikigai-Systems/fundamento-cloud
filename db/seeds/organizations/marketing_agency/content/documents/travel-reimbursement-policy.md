# Travel Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose

This policy covers expense reimbursement for work-related travel, including client meetings, industry events, and team offsites. BrightPath will reimburse reasonable expenses incurred while traveling on company business.

## Eligible Expense Categories

### Flights
- Book economy class for domestic flights
- Business class requires pre-approval for flights over 5 hours
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible to secure better rates

### Hotels
- Maximum **$200 per night** including taxes and fees
- Use preferred hotel partners when available (list in Slack #travel channel)
- For stays longer than 3 nights, consider Airbnb as a cost-effective alternative

### Meals
- Maximum **$75 per day** for meals while traveling
- This covers breakfast, lunch, dinner, and reasonable tips
- Team dinners with clients may exceed this limit with prior manager approval

### Ground Transportation
- Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) and taxis for airport transfers and client meetings
- Rental cars require pre-approval and should be economy or mid-size class
- Mileage for personal vehicle use is reimbursed at the current IRS rate

## Submission Process

1. Collect and photograph all receipts during your trip
2. Submit an expense report **within 30 days** of the travel date
3. Include itemized receipts for every expense over $25
4. Add a brief note explaining the business purpose of each expense
5. Submit through the Expense Reports table in the BrightPath HQ space

- Expenses under $500: Approved by your direct manager

## What Is Not Covered

- Alcohol (unless part of a client dinner with prior approval)
- Personal entertainment or sightseeing
- Travel insurance (covered by the company policy)
- Upgrades or premium services without pre-approval

## Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are reimbursed within **10 business days** via direct deposit.
