When you are logged-in, just click on your initials on the main menu (top-right in the work area)

![MC.jpg](attachment:444)

Then choose: Teams

![Create Team1.jpg](attachment:445)

Then click "Create Team" button

![Create Team2.jpg](attachment:446)

And you will see area where you should name your Team, create shortcut and search for users that should be in that particular Team.

![Create Team3.jpg](attachment:447)

Remember to fill all required fields. e.g. as a Shortcut try to use a word that you would like the document to be searched for later by our search engine.

After you have named Team, created Shorcut and picked Team member just click "Create" button below.
