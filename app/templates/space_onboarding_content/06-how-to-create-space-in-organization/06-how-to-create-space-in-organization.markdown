Spaces organize your Documents and Tables into tree-like structures. Different people in your Organization can have different access permissions to your Spaces.

In order to create a new Space choose on the left side of the page a select button next to name of your Organization and then click "Create space"

![Create space1.jpg](attachment:439)

Name this space and choose a mode access to this space.

When you pick "public" - every person from the Organization will have an access to this space.

As you choose "restricted" - every document in this particular space will be **visible** to anyone within organization, but editable only to space managers or organization managers.

When you decide on "private" - every document in this particular space will be **visible** and **editable** only to space managers or organization managers

![Create space2.jpg](attachment:440)

In the last step you have to find and confirm who from your Organization should have an access to this specific space.

![Create space3.jpg](attachment:441)

As you have chosen a Team member with an access to this space, just click "Create" button

![Create space4.jpg](attachment:442)

In the next step you'll see that a new space has been successfuly created and you are in the "start view"

![List of spaces.jpg](attachment:443)
