In order to create an Organization - when you are logged-in, just click on your initials on the main menu (top-right in the work area)

![MC.jpg](attachment:433)

Then choose: Organizations

![Choose Organizations.jpg](attachment:434)

click "Create" button

![create.jpg](attachment:435)

Add a name of the Organization and click "Create"

![create2.jpg](attachment:436)

After creating new Organization you can see current status. Here your role is being shown, you can change it in this area. You are able now to add/invite new users to this particular organization (explanations below)

Important: when you want to start work within a new Organization, click "Switch to" button

![admin organization.jpg](attachment:437)

then you will see "Start view" and you can work in that space

![BlockNote image](https://fundamento.cloud/attachments/194)

With the intention of delete an Organization you are supposed to click on your initials on the main menu:

![MC.jpg](attachment:502)

choose: Organizations

![Choose Organizations.jpg](attachment:503)

then choose: "show" or "click to show" (depending on what Organization your're logged in at the moment) to see view of details of your Organization.

![delete Organization.jpg](attachment:504)

Use a symbol down arrow to and click on it to see the possibillity deleting the Organization

![Delete Org 2.jpg](attachment:506)

Before permanent delete, we are going to ask you whether you are sure about this kind of action via a message: "*Are you sure? Your organization will be immediately removed!"*

Then you have to just confirm it or abandon this action.

Suppose that you would like only to change name of your Organization, click "edit" button

![Edit Organization1.jpg](attachment:509)

and now you are able to change the name of the Organization, do it and click "Save" button

![Edit Organization2.jpg](attachment:510)

"Switch to" button applies also here as you want to open a workspace of this particular Organization, since you could be in the "show Organization" mode at the moment. Use it when you want to start or continue work in one of the Spaces of this particular Organization.

![Switch to Organization.jpg](attachment:511)
